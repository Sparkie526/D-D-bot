=== TITLE: The Curse of Ashmore Keep ===


=== SETTING ===
A dark fantasy world. The town of Millhaven sits on the edge of a cursed forest.
The locals are superstitious and fearful. Strange disappearances have been happening for weeks.
The weather is always overcast. Ravens watch from the rooftops.


=== CURRENT QUEST ===
The players have been hired by Elder Brynn to investigate Ashmore Keep, an abandoned fortress
on the hill north of Millhaven. Townsfolk say lights have been seen there at night.
The real secret (players don't know yet): A necromancer named Serafin has taken up residence
and is raising the old soldiers buried in the keep's catacombs.


=== MAP — MILLHAVEN TOWN ===

THE INN (The Broken Antler)
  A warm, smoky inn. Mira the innkeeper runs it.
  Exits: Front door to the Main Street. Back door to the alley.
  Notable: A noticeboard with missing persons flyers. A drunk guard named Pol sleeps in the corner.

MAIN STREET
  Connects: The Inn to the north, the Blacksmith to the east, the Town Gate to the north.
  Notable: A well in the center. Townspeople avoid eye contact with strangers.

BLACKSMITH (Aldric's Forge)
  A large stone building, always warm.
  Exits: Main Street.
  Notable: Aldric is here during the day. Closed at night.


=== MAP — ASHMORE KEEP ===

ROOM 1 - The Gatehouse
  Crumbling stone walls. Iron portcullis is jammed open.
  Exits: South leads back to the forest path. North leads to the Courtyard.
  Contains: Two skeletons in rusted armor (not animated, just remains). A broken cart.
  Secrets: None.

ROOM 2 - The Courtyard
  Open to the sky. Overgrown with weeds. A dry fountain in the center.
  Exits: North leads to the Main Hall. East leads to the Barracks. West leads to the Chapel.
  Contains: 2 skeleton guards patrolling (hostile). A well (dried up, 20ft deep).
  Secrets: The well has iron rungs going down — leads to the Catacombs (Room 6).

ROOM 3 - The Main Hall
  Grand but ruined. A long table, rotted. Tattered banners on the walls.
  Exits: South to Courtyard. Stairs up to the Tower (Room 5). Door north to Serafin's Chamber (Room 4, locked).
  Contains: 3 skeleton warriors (hostile). A chest with 40 gold pieces and a healing potion.
  Secrets: One banner hides a switch that unlocks Room 4 without needing the key.

ROOM 4 - Serafin's Chamber (LOCKED — needs key or the hidden switch in Room 3)
  Cluttered with books, candles, alchemical equipment. Smells of sulfur.
  Exits: South to Main Hall.
  Contains: SERAFIN THE NECROMANCER (boss fight). His spellbook. A crystal orb glowing green.
  Secrets: The crystal orb is the source of the curse. Destroying it ends the necromancy.
  Serafin's weakness: He cannot cast spells if silenced. He will try to flee if below half health.

ROOM 5 - The Tower (top floor)
  Windy. Panoramic view of the forest and Millhaven below.
  Exits: Stairs down to Main Hall.
  Contains: An old telescope. A journal from the keep's last captain (reveals Serafin's history).
  Secrets: A loose stone hides the key to Room 4.

ROOM 6 - The Catacombs (accessed via well in Courtyard)
  Dark, damp, and narrow. Torchlight flickers.
  Exits: Iron rungs up to the Courtyard well.
  Contains: 20 coffins. 4 are open and empty (those skeletons are now upstairs).
  Secrets: One coffin contains the skeleton of Lord Ashmore himself — he is NOT hostile and
  will speak to the players if awakened with a Speak with Dead spell. He will warn them
  about Serafin and beg them to destroy the crystal orb.


=== NPCS ===

MIRA (Innkeeper, The Broken Antler)
  Personality: Cautious, motherly, tired. Distrusts outsiders but warms up quickly.
  Knows: The disappearances started 3 weeks ago. People who go near the Keep don't come back.
  Secret: Her son was one of the first to disappear. She suspects the Keep but is too afraid to say.
  Will offer: Free room and board if players agree to find out what happened to her son.

ALDRIC (Blacksmith)
  Personality: Gruff, honest, respects strength. Short sentences.
  Knows: He's been secretly supplying Serafin with iron for "building projects" — he didn't ask questions.
  Secret: He is guilt-ridden and suspects something is wrong. He won't admit this unless pressed hard.
  Will offer: Weapon repairs at half price. Will give players a silver sword if they promise to stop Serafin.

ELDER BRYNN (Quest giver)
  Personality: Old, formal, nervous. Speaks in long sentences.
  Knows: The history of Ashmore Keep. That a scholar named Serafin rented the keep 2 months ago.
  Secret: He paid Serafin to research a cure for a plague — he didn't know Serafin would turn to necromancy.
  Will offer: 200 gold on completion of the quest.

POL (Drunk guard at the inn)
  Personality: Bumbling, chatty when drunk, surprisingly brave when sober.
  Knows: He saw a green light at the top of the Keep's tower last Tuesday night.
  Will offer: To join the party as a hireling (cheap, unreliable, occasionally heroic).


=== HOUSE RULES ===
- Critical hits deal maximum damage on both dice rolls
- Players can attempt any creative action even without a matching skill
- If a player roleplay action is clever enough, the DM may grant advantage without a roll
- Death saving throws: players are not out of the game on 3 failures — they are unconscious and
  can be revived by another player spending an action


=== TONE NOTES FOR THE DM ===
- This campaign leans gothic horror — describe things as unsettling, cold, and foreboding
- NPCs are scared and desperate, not heroic
- Reward clever thinking and roleplay, not just combat
- The necromancer Serafin is not purely evil — he believed he was doing good research. 
  Players can choose to talk him down instead of fighting him if they are persuasive enough.
